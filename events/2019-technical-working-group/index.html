2019 Technical Working Group Meeting

Inaugural December 2019 meeting of the I3 TWG

workshop
technical-working-group
featured
Inaugural two-day meeting in Cambridge featuring initial data construction projects including prior art metrics, Public Innovation Dataverse, patent-science references, and product-patent linking. Panel discussions covered disambiguation challenges, text analysis applications, and major patent data infrastructure efforts.
